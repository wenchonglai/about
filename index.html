As an urban designer, I accumulate critical thinking and quantitative analysis skills, get inspired for creative IT solutions to urban issues, and implement innovative ideas though day-to-day practices in legal documents reading, site planning, design feasibility studies, design standards drafting, as well as collaboration with diverse team and community members.

Make story-telling interactive maps.

Since 2013, I have started making web applications for interactive maps using JavaScript, jQuery, SVG, Canvas, and React. Compared to the static maps that planners conventionally produce, these interactive maps were much more powerful in conveying comprehensive, informative, and personalized messages tailored to each and every individual amongst thousands of recipients. These interactive maps were also proven to be more cost-efficient, as the web-based approach eliminates the countless redraws incurred during design iterations.
